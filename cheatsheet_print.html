Examiner Q&A — quick answers

Works on any EEG machine?
No — needs the same format (single-channel, 178 samples/sec). Other devices need resampling first. The 🎲 button uses real clips in this exact format.
Not overfitting?
Stratified train/val/test split, fixed seed, scaler fit on train only (no leakage), early stopping. All numbers are on the held-out test set.
Class imbalance (20% seizure)?
Handled with class weighting; I report seizure recall & precision, not just accuracy.
Why CNN and LSTM?
CNN = local spike shapes; LSTM = temporal pattern. Baseline AUC 0.9952 vs deep 0.9988 shows it helps.
Improve with more time?
Patient-independent splits, k-fold CV, spectrogram features, 5-class version, TensorFlow Lite for wearables.
Real-world use?
Wearable seizure alerts, assisting neurologists, long-term monitoring.
